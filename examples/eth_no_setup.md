The institutional context shows mild accumulation for ETH on a macro level, but that doesn't override the technical reality on the 15-minute timeframe. Macro context provides background; it doesn't manufacture setups where none exist.

**This is the kind of output that matters most.** Any system can tell you to buy when indicators are screaming. The real test of a framework is whether it can tell you to sit on your hands when the market is ambiguous. Most bots can't do that. This one can.
